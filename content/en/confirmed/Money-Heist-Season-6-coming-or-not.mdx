## Why Netflix Chose Spin-offs Over Season 6

From a production standpoint, the decision regarding whether **Money Heist Season 6 coming or not** was likely influenced by the desire for longevity. By ending *La Casa de Papel* at Part 5, the creators preserved the integrity of the characters' arcs.

The shift to the *Berlin* series and other spin-offs allows Netflix to:
*   **Explore different genres:** The *Berlin* spin-off feels more like a romantic heist-comedy compared to the gritty, war-like atmosphere of the Bank of Spain heist.

*   **Attract new audiences:** Remakes like the Korean version allow the story to reach viewers who might prefer local actors and localized political themes.
